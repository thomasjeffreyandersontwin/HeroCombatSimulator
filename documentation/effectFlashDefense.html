Effect Overview

Effect Flash Defense

Description

Works in conjunction with powerFlashDefense and effectFlash to reduce the duration of a flash effect.

Functional Overview

EffectFlashDefense is a passive effect.  EffectFlash checks for the existance of flash defense and modifies itself appropriately.

Known Caveats

None.
