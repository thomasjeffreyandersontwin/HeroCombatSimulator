Power Overview

Power Damage Resistance

Description

Implements the Damage Resistance power.  The amount of rPD and rED resistance is configurable.

Functional Overview

When activated, an standard effect is applied to the source.  The effect has two subeffect:  one for AID to rPD and one for AID to rED.

Known Caveats

None.
