Power Overview

Power MentalDefense

Description

Implements Mental Defense.  Number of points of MD are configurable.

Functional Overview

When activated Mental Defense executes a subeffect of "aid" to add the number of points of mental defense plus EGO/5 to the MD stat.

Known Caveats

-Since powerMentalDefense handles adding EGO/5 to the MD stat, activating an instance of powerCharacteristic with EGO will not add to the source's MD. Deactivating and reactivating the Mental Defense will force the MD to be calculated correctly.
