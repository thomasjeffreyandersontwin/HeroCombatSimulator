Effect Overview

Effect DamageReduction

Description

Reduces the amount of damage applied to the character by a certain percent.  Used by power Damage Reduction to produce appropriate effects.

Functional Overview

Uses the postdefense method to reduced all subeffect with the configured type of Defense, by the configured percent.

Known Caveats

Needs to be check to make sure that both body and killing of all special defense types (Killing, NND, AVLD) work appropraitely.
Currently, all subeffects are adjusted, including aids, drains, and sets.  However, if they do not have a defense applied, they are not adjusted.
