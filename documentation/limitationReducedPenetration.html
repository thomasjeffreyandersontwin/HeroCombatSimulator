Limitation Overview

Limitation Reduced Penetration

Description

Implements Reduced Penetration.  There are no configurable parameters.

Functional Overview

Uses postpower to recalculate the amount of damage which penetrates the defenses of the target.  Specifically, if B = original body damage, and D = defense, then final damage should be B-2D.  Note:  Damage is still applied as a single effect.

Known Caveats

Only works with powers which apply Body damage in a stat metric (not statcp's).
Most likely, knockback calculations will be incorrect.
Other advantages, limitations, and effects which depend on the original body may be skewed by the change in final body damage.
