Power Overview

Power Force Field

Description

Implements Force Field power.  Configurable for the amount of PD and amount of ED applied.

Functional Overview

When power is triggered, power generates effectDefenseModifier with appropriate PD/ED values.  Effect stays in effect until power is shut down.

Known Caveats

None.
