Power Overview

Power Hand-to-Hand Killing Attack

Description

Implements the standard Hand to Hand Killing Attack Power.  Amount of damage, in D6 notation, and defense (PD,ED) is configurable.

Functional Overview

Uses the standard BattleEngine damage generation.

Known Caveats

None.
