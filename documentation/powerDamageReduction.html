Power Overview

Power Damage Reduction

Description

Implements power damage reduction.  The percent reduction, type (PD/ED), and resistance of the damage reduction are configurable.

Functional Overview

When applied to source, creates effectDamageReduction which watches for all Damage subeffects applied to the character, and reduces them appropriately.

Known Caveats

May conflict with Penetrating.
May not correctly reflect the resistant damage condition.
